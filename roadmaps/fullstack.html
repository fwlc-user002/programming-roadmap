🚀 Fullstack Development Roadmap

🎨 Color Legend

  • 🟣 - Core technologies: Essential skills and tools that every fullstack developer must learn.
  • 🟢 - Advanced concepts: More specialized or optional technologies that improve efficiency and scalability.
  • 🔵 - DevOps & Deployment: Tools and practices related to deploying and maintaining applications.

This roadmap will guide you through the essential skills and technologies needed to become a fullstack developer.

📖 Introduction

Fullstack development involves both frontend and backend development, allowing the developer to work on the entire application.
